Our Platform's main goal

Kanishk Gupta with the cofounder Aditya Kumar Srivastava founded the company Learning beyond your horizon CBSE in the year 2022. He has previous experience working in prestigious consulting, educational, and technological organizations and is well-equipped with the best industry experiences to give each student a sincere effort for their study using cutting-edge technology. We work hard and ensure that we provide the highest-quality education at the lowest feasible price. To ensure that every student obtains a personalized learning experience from India's top professors, we put all of our efforts into ensuring that each student receives the personal attention from teachers and academic mentors that is highly important for the maximum learning of each student. Learning beyond your horizon CBSE is an online educational platform that equips children with 21st-century skills that are lacking or grossly underestimated in the majority of current educational systems but are crucial to our children's success in the long run. Learning can significantly impact how a child's life is shaped, especially as the world moves inexorably toward digitalization. We at Learning beyond your horizon CBSE take this element seriously and work hard to improve our strategy for online learning by using the following techniques. .

What Makes us Different?

At Learning Beyond Your Horizon CBSE, we believe that using digital technology will assist students to receive individualized instruction and care from India's top math and science instructors. We are committed to building a world-class e-learning platform that unites the top professors, media, and content to give every student a seamless and exceptional learning experience.
